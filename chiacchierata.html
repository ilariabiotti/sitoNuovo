Chiacchierata

digital HD video, color, sound. 5'48". Ventotene (IT). 2017
Chiacchierata a video and a postcard serie. A group of mythological invisible beings, the Sirens of Ventotene, interacting with a found object, one of the many smartphones that fall from Ventotene's cliffs.
Commissioned by IN/SU/LA Ventotene Contemporary
